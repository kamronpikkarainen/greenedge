FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before booking.

How do I get a quote?
Fill out the contact form below with your yard details and we'll reply within 24 hours with a flat-rate quote. No obligation, no pressure — just a number you can say yes or no to.
Do I need to be home when you come?
No — most of our customers aren't home. As long as we can access your yard, we'll handle everything and send you a message or photo once we're done.
What if it rains on my scheduled day?
We reschedule for the next available slot and let you know in advance. We don't mow wet lawns — it tears up turf and leaves a mess. Your lawn always comes first.
Is there a contract or commitment?
None. You can cancel or pause service anytime with no penalties. We earn your business every visit — not by locking you in.
What areas do you serve?
We serve Raleigh, Durham, Cary, Chapel Hill, Wake Forest, Apex, Charlotte, and surrounding areas across North Carolina. If you're not sure, just ask — we're expanding all the time.
Do you offer recurring service?
Absolutely. Weekly and bi-weekly plans are available and tend to produce the best-looking lawns. Recurring customers get priority scheduling and consistent crews.
